Paragraph 1
The story starts at the end of high school for our Bob White, it's a summer day like any other, he sits on his parent's house poarch, eating icecream. As the sun shines on his balding head, he thinks about his next years in life, he has a future-altering decission to make: should he follow on his studies and go to university or should he try to make it big in this cruel world on his own.

Paragraph 2
He ultimately chose to have a higher education. And so, he packed his bags and got ready for uni life, in Computer Science. He's not exactly the brightest, but promised himself to take care of his studies. On the first day, some friendly students approached him, offering him a cigarette. Bob ain't EVER smoked before in his life, but he wanted to be with the cool kids.
Go to paragraph 4 for choosing to smoke
Go to paragraph 5 for refusing the cig

Paragraph 5
At that moment a battle started inside of him, should he try to smoke for the first time and befriend the cool kids or should he keep his promise to himself to never smoke again after his father died from lung cancer. In the end he refused and went to the uni library to complete his assignments. From this point, he managed to follow the correct path his whole life. 10 years have passed the incident, meanwhile he completed his phd in AI Research and managed to find a really high payed job which we also enjoys. After all those 10 years he looked to the sky thinking about his father and a tear started to go down on his cheek.
