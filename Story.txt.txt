Paragraph 1

Paragraph 7
He chose to start his new life as a Bombardier in his neighbourhood. At first he was just an arrow selling drugs for the big bosses. With his first "paycheck" he started to spend money on underage girls and a bmw e46 from 1998. After a while he started climing the hierarchy. In 5 years he made more money that your whole family will make in their lifetime. After 10 more years the party was over, the police got him and he got 30 years in prison for multiple crimes. After he servbed his sentance he tried to make a new life but unfortunately nobody would hire him and he ended up dying from an overdose of meth, such a miserable life...
